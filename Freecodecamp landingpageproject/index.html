EQ - Electric Intelligence.

The new brand for electric mobility

The future is electric and intelligent. Intelligent electric mobility - this is what the new Mercedes-Benz EQ product brand stands for. An attractive design, exceptional driving fun, high levels of suitability for everyday use and maximum safety are further attributes. EQ represents the two Mercedes-Benz brand values of "emotion and intelligence" and comprises all significant aspects of customer-oriented electric mobility..

Preview of a new generation of vehicles with battery-electric drives.

The Concept EQ heralded the launch of the new EQ product and technology brand and gave first in 2016 a preview of a whole generation of electric vehicles. The Mercedes-Benz Concept EQA, like the smart vision EQ fortwo enjoyed its world premiere at the 2017 International Motor Show. The youngest member of the EQ concept car family, the Vision EQ Silver Arrow embodies progressive luxury and drives the design idiom of EQ to the top.
